## DESIGN GUIDELINES FOR POWERPOINT:

### Color Palette:
- **Primary:** #5E72E4 (Blue) or #6366F1 (Indigo)
- **Accent:** #F97316 (Orange) or #FB923C (Peach)
- **Background:** #FFFFFF (White) or #F8FAFC (Light Gray)
- **Text:** #1E293B (Dark Gray) or #0F172A (Near Black)

### Typography:
- **Headers:** Montserrat Bold or Poppins Bold (24-36pt)
- **Body:** Open Sans or Inter Regular (14-18pt)
- **Code/Technical:** Consolas or Courier New (12-14pt)

### Visual Elements:
- Use icons from Font Awesome or similar
- Include screenshots of the web interface
- Add charts/graphs for performance metrics
- Use subtle gradients and shadows
- Maintain consistent spacing (1.5x line height)

### Slide Transitions:
- Use "Fade" or "Morph" for smooth transitions
- Keep animations subtle and professional
- Avoid excessive animation effects
